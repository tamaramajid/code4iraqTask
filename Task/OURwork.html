OUR MISSION
Iraq Child is a non-profit organization working to protect child rights and help families out of the poverty cycle through education, healthcare, and income generating opportunities. Iraq Child also connects Iraqi youth to less fortunate children in other countries, educating them about the challenges facing children and inspiring them to take leadership in humanitarian causes.
